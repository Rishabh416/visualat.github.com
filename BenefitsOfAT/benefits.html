Benefits of Visual Assistive Technology

Assistive Technology is greatly used around the world because of the ease it brings. These products make life easier in many ways. Physical products such as walking sticks make it easier for people to move around. Some people may find it difficult to read or see. Braille and spectacles/glasses are assistive products because it helps a person read or see clearly.

  • Glasses/lenses help people see clearly.
  • Canes ease movement.
  • Tinted glasses reverse color blindness.
  • Braille can help blind people read letters and words.
  • Text-to-Speech reads content out loud
